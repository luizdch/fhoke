A short story of an independent creative & innovative design studio.

Fhoke Studio is a small-sized team of young, creative professionals based out of India. We have been exploring & designing digital beauty since 2009 and delivered remarkable projects for the big and small clients.

We were the partnership of three individuals with different backgrounds and different locations, and form a team for new challenges, where we can collaborate with people and companies to get their product ideas rolling. Our team can deliver everything from idea to final execution.

We’re offering multiple digital services including, web design, app design, interaction design, UI/UX, icon design, graphic design, illustration, branding, packaging, print design, logo Design, motion graphics, video editing, web development (WordPress), HTML, CSS & Hosting. Our philosophy is coming up with fresh ideas and keep things simple.
